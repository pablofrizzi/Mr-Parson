The truth about Mr. Parsons

FACT 1

FACT 2

After a brief involvement with Marxism in 1939, Parsons converted to Thelema, the English occultist Aleister Crowley's new religious movement. In 1941, alongside his first wife Helen Northrup, Parsons joined the Agape Lodge, the Californian branch of the Thelemite Ordo Templi Orientis (O.T.O.). At Crowley's bidding, he replaced Wilfred Talbot Smith as its leader in 1942 and ran the Lodge from his mansion on Orange Grove Avenue.

FACT 3

Parsons was employed by North American Aviation at Inglewood, where he worked on the Navaho Missile Program.

FACT 4

The International Astronomical Union decided to name a crater on the far side of the Moon Parsons after him in 1972.
